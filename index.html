ABOUT US

My husband and I started Nevah Coffee Co
in early 2023 after being married for a
few short years. We've always had a passion
to serve the community and
(in true Seattleite fashion)
have always loved to have a good cup of coffee.

Nevah began as a small dream to share
our love for the drink with the people
around us and quickly turned into a
full-time coffee catering service.
It's the perfect marriage of the two things
that we love the most:
meeting people's needs and enjoying good coffee!
